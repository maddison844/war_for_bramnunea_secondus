Northern Provinces



The Northern Province - Ecclesiastical Heart. Oldest of the Provinces.
The Northern Province is the cradle of Branunea Secondus — the first region settled, the first fortified, and the first to swear fealty during Imperial Compliance. Its coastline is temperate and fertile, its highlands defensible, and its cities ancient enough that their foundations predate recorded history. From this land, the Planetary Governor rules with the weight of millennia behind him, supported by the Administratum’s vast bureaucratic engines and the unyielding presence of the Ecclesiarchy.
Faith and governance intertwine here. The Governor’s decrees are issued from Viridianus Magna, but they are legitimised by the sermons and proclamations of the Cardinal‑Prefect in Saint Biffobrin Reach. Pilgrims, tithe‑clerks, regimental musters, and noble delegations all pass through the North, making it the most tightly controlled and symbolically important territory on the planet.
Though the saint’s martyrdom occurred far to the south at Saint’s End, it was the Northern Province that became the custodian of his legacy. The procession that carried his body north reshaped the province forever, transforming it into a beacon of Imperial devotion. Today, the Northern Province stands as both the mind and soul of Branunea Secondus — a land where every field, shrine, and fortress whispers of duty, sacrifice, and the Emperor’s eternal gaze.



After the Rift


When the Cicatrix Maledictum tore the galaxy asunder, Branunea Secondus found itself stranded in Imperium Nihilus, cut off from the light of the Astronomican. The Northern Province, once the most orderly and faithful region of the world, became a crucible of endurance. Astropathic communication faltered, Administratum directives ceased, and the Ecclesiarchy’s prayers grew desperate.
Yet the North endured. The Basilica of Saint Biffobrin declared the saint’s incorrupt body a living conduit of the Emperor’s will, and his shrine became a psychic beacon for the faithful. The Governor’s council re‑established planetary governance under candlelight and vox‑relay, and the people learned to live by faith rather than decree. The Northern Province now stands as a fortress of belief, its citizens convinced that their survival above the Rift is proof of divine favour.
