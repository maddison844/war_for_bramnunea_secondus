War For Bramnunea

Widow’s Coast — The Cliffside Port of Duty and Departure



Widow’s Coast is a rugged stretch of shoreline ending in a sheer cliff face overlooking the ocean. Atop this cliff sits the Widow’s Coast Spaceport, the largest off‑world export hub on Branunea Secondus. From here, bulk lifters carry lumber from the western forests, ore from the frontier mines, refined materials from the manufactoria, and processed foodstuffs from Viridianus Magna to orbiting cargo haulers.
Its name is a grim one: this is the muster point for newly raised Imperial Guard regiments. Families gather on the cliff’s edge to watch their loved ones march into the transports, many never to return. The winds that sweep the cliffs are said to echo the cries of those left behind, giving the region its mournful name. Memorial walls line the approach to the spaceport, engraved with the names of regiments raised here — some honoured, some lost, some never heard from again.



After the Rift


The Rift’s opening severed all stable warp routes, trapping Branunea Secondus in isolation. The spaceport fell silent; its launch bays became mausoleums of grounded transports. The Widow’s Coast Navy repurposed its vessels for orbital defence, and the cliffs became a fortress line against potential incursions.
Yet even in isolation, the Coast retained its purpose. Regiments still muster here — not for off‑world campaigns, but for planetary defence. The memorial walls continue to grow, now bearing the names of those who fell defending Branunea Secondus itself. The people say the Emperor still hears their vows, even through the Rift’s storm, and that every departing soldier’s soul reaches Him across the void.
